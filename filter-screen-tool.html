Photoetch manufacturing checks
Assumes double-sided etch on stainless steel. Standard rules of thumb: hole diameter ≥ 1.2× material thickness, web width (gap) ≥ 1× thickness, etch ratio (thickness ÷ hole) ≤ 1.0, open area 20–50% for structural integrity. Always confirm exact tolerances with your photoetch supplier before committing to film.
